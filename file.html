Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the Frequently asked questions

What is Entrepreneurship?

Entrepreneurship is the act of creating, developing, and managing a new business venture to make a profit by taking financial risks. It involves identifying opportunities, innovating, and driving growth in the market. Entrepreneurs are individuals who pursue these opportunities and transform ideas into viable businesses.

Who is Entrepreneur?

An entrepreneur is someone who creates, develops, and manages a new business venture, taking on financial risks in the hope of making a profit. Entrepreneurs are innovative thinkers who identify opportunities and turn ideas into successful businesses.

Difference Between Entrepreneurship and Entrepreneur?

Entrepreneurship: The process of starting and running a business.
Entrepreneur: The person who starts and runs the business.
